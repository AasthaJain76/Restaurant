Welcome to Jai Jinendra Sa


Journey has been started by Mr. Ashish Jain in the year 1987 from the restaurant at railway station Road Mandsaur. With the determination, willingness, and hard work of JJS family, today we are successfully running 3 branches at prime locations of Mandsaur.

The leading vegetarian food chain in Central India, with promise of quality and taste is famous for its exotic spices, delicate herbs, and fine dining experience. We offer wide range in our menu that includes Punjabi, South Indian, North Indian, Chinese, Continental, Chaats and beverages. Foodies love some of our signature mouthwatering and lip-smacking delicacies.
Today we are the first choice of foodies among all the restaurants in Mandsaur. We prepare our food fresh and with love as per the need of our customers. In order to maintain the finest quality and taste, best and authentic ingredients are delivered to our restaurants daily. Being the best restaurant in MP, Jai Jinendra Sa Restaurant is committed to provide excellent food, high standard of service, and an unforgettable
dining experience for you and your family to enjoy every day and many years to come.
